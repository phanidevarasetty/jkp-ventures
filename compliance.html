Compliance & Governance

Last updated: December 2025

JKP Ventures is committed to operating in accordance with applicable laws, regulations, and internationally recognised standards of corporate governance, ethics, and risk management. This page outlines our compliance framework and operational principles.

1. Corporate Identity

2. Regulatory Compliance

We conduct our activities in compliance with all applicable UAE laws and regulations, including commercial, intellectual property, technology, and data protection frameworks.

Where our activities involve international counterparties, we take reasonable steps to align with relevant cross-border regulatory requirements.

3. Intellectual Property Governance

  • Ownership, licensing, and enforcement of proprietary software and data assets
  • Protection of patents, trade secrets, copyrights, and trademarks
  • Contractual safeguards governing IP use by partners and licensees

4. Data Protection & Privacy

We maintain internal controls designed to protect personal and confidential data. Our data-handling practices align with applicable privacy laws and are described in detail in our Privacy Policy.

5. Information Security

We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to safeguard systems, software, and data against unauthorised access, misuse, or disruption.

6. Anti-Bribery & Ethical Conduct

JKP Ventures maintains a zero-tolerance approach to bribery, corruption, and unethical conduct. All business dealings are expected to be conducted with integrity, transparency, and accountability.

7. Risk Management

We continuously assess operational, legal, technological, and reputational risks and implement appropriate mitigation strategies proportionate to our business activities.

8. Third-Party Relationships

Partners, vendors, and licensees are expected to adhere to contractual obligations and applicable legal standards. We reserve the right to review and terminate relationships that present unacceptable compliance risks.

9. Reporting & Oversight

Compliance matters are overseen at the management level. Where required, we cooperate fully with lawful requests from regulatory or governmental authorities.

10. Updates to This Page

This compliance statement may be updated periodically to reflect changes in law, regulation, or business operations. Updates will be posted on this page.
